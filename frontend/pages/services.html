Benefits of Composting

Reduces Carbon Footprint

By composting, you help reduce methane emissions from landfills and lower your carbon footprint.

Enriches Soil

Compost adds essential nutrients to soil, improves soil structure, and helps retain moisture for plants.

Reduces Waste

Up to 30% of household waste can be composted, reducing landfill use and waste management costs.
